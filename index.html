Privacy Policy for Tensor

Effective date: January 2026

Tensor (“we”, “us”, or “the App”) provides a barcode scanning and product search tool to find price matches from publicly available web listings.

Information We Collect

Tensor does not require an account and does not collect personal data such as names, email addresses, or payment information by default.

We may process:

  • Search text you enter.
  • Barcode (UPC/EAN) scanned via your camera.
  • Basic network metadata necessary for API requests (e.g., IP address) processed by our hosting provider.

Use of Information

Information is used only to perform price lookup and return results to you. We do not store personal information with the intent to identify individuals.

Camera Access

Tensors requests camera access only to enable barcode scanning. The live camera feed is used only to detect barcodes and is not recorded or stored.

Third-Party Sharing

If you tap a “Buy” link, you are redirected to the respective merchant site. That site’s privacy policy then applies.

We use our hosting provider’s infrastructure to serve API data, which may process requests in accordance with its own infrastructure policies.

Data Retention

Tensor does not intentionally retain search or barcode data. Temporary logs may be retained by infrastructure providers for security and reliability.

Analytics

Tensor does not include third-party analytics or tracking SDKs at this time.

Children

This App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
